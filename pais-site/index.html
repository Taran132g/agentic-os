FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does an engagement work?

We start with a call to map your operations, then design and build the agent workflows that fit — your inboxes, your pipeline, your tools. You watch them run in your own Control Room before anything goes live.

How does it know our business?

Your docs and knowledge become a private vector brain the agents query — so they draft in your voice, know your customers, and follow your playbooks. No generic templates.

Is my data private?

Your notes become a private vector brain scoped to your account. Agents run on your own machine with your own accounts — your data stays on hardware you control.

What do agents do?

They do real work and message you on the site: brief your day, triage your inbox, scout and qualify leads, draft outreach, sync your repos, and audit your routine.

What does it cost?

Scoped per engagement — it depends on how many workflows we build and what they touch. Book a call and you'll have a number before we hang up.

Do I need to babysit it?

No. Set your team up once, connect your desktop, and they run on a schedule — reporting back to your Control Room.
