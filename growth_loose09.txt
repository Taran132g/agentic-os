There's a version of you that scrolls at 1am, watching everyone your age get the job, the relationship, the whole life... and quietly wonders what's wrong with him.
You won't say that out loud. Guys don't. You'll like the post, type "congrats bro," and fall asleep feeling a little further behind than you did yesterday.
So let me say the part you keep swallowing.
It's not that you're lazy. It's not that something's broken in you. It's that nobody ever told you the timeline you're measuring yourself against was invented.
Half the guys you compare yourself to feel exactly the way you do right now. The confident ones too. Especially them.
Feeling behind isn't proof you failed. It's proof you actually care where your life is going. And that caring is the raw material everything good gets built from.
You don't fix this by suddenly having it all figured out. You pick one small thing that's yours, and you do it today, before you feel ready.
Not for the timeline. Not for the post. For the guy lying awake at 1am who needs to watch you try.
You're not behind. You're early in a story you keep judging by its first chapter.
