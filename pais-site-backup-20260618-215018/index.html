        FAQ

        Questions, answered.

        Do I need to be techy or install anything?

        No. We do the setup on a call. There's no new software to learn — you get a simple screen on your phone where you tap "send" or "skip." That's it.

        Will it text or call my customers without me knowing?

        Never. PAIS only ever drafts the message. Nothing goes out until you approve it. You're always the one who decides what your customers hear.

        How does it sound like my business?

        We set it up with your hours, your menu or services, and your tone — so a text or a phone answer sounds like you, not a robot. No generic scripts.

        Is my customer list safe?

        Yes. It runs on your own accounts and stays private to your business. We don't sell or share your customer data — it's only used to help you keep them.

        What if I already use a booking or POS system?

        Great — we connect to what you already have (Square, Toast, your booking system, or even a simple customer list). You don't switch anything.

        What does it cost?

        It depends on how much you want it to handle. Book a call and you'll have a clear number before we hang up — no surprises, no lock-in.
