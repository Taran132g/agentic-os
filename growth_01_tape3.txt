Sitting at that sunset, I could feel every generation before me. Farmers. Sailors. My own ancestors. All of them watched this exact same sun.
And for the first time in my life, I understood my place on this earth.
Then a quote came back to me. Steve Jobs said the most profound experiences of his life showed him what actually mattered. Not making money. Creating great things, and, in his exact words, putting things back into the stream of history and of human consciousness.
And that's when it clicked.
