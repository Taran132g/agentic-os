So here's what I actually did about the seven out of ten.
I didn't delete everything and move to the woods. Let's be real. But I went back and read what the AI actually told me, and I noticed it had given me two completely different things.
It gave me a score. Which I can't control, and which measures me against every other face on earth. And it gave me levers. Sleep. Training. Grooming. Showing up. Which I fully control, and which only measure me against yesterday.
The score is their game. The levers are mine. So I stopped tracking how I ranked and started tracking what I did. Nights I slept. Workouts I finished. Promises to myself I actually kept.
And the machine turned out to be right about one thing. The softness was never genetic. Neither was the doubt.
So do one thing for me. The next time you catch yourself about to ask a screen what you're worth, close it, and go do one rep of something instead. That's the only rating that ever moves.
