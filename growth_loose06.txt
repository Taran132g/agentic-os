Have you noticed... you're a different person at 2am.
In the daytime, you've got it together. You laugh at the right moments. You answer "how are you" correctly. The act runs itself.
Then the house goes quiet. The phone dims. And the questions show up — the ones with no business being answered at 2am.
Am I doing enough. Do they actually like me. Is this the life I wanted... or just the one I got used to.
My 2am questions don't match my 2pm personality. And for a long time, I thought that meant something was wrong with me.
But the night isn't creating those thoughts. It's just turning the noise off... so you can finally hear them.
The daytime you is who you perform. The 2am you is who you protect. Both are real. Only one is honest.
So don't fear the quiet. The things that find you at night are just the parts of you... asking to be taken seriously.
Listen to them in the dark. Then do something about them in the light.
