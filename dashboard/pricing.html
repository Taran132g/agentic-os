Pricing FAQ

The honest answers.

Why is the pilot free?

Because you shouldn't pay for something you haven't seen work. We run it against your real customers for 30 days — if it doesn't bring bookings and regulars back, you walk, no charge.

What does the monthly actually cover?

Us running and maintaining the workflows, the integrations, and the Control Room — plus the model usage. One recovered booking a week usually covers it several times over.

Is there a setup fee?

Not for founding clients. Later clients may have a one-time setup; founding rate-holders never do.

Do messages go out without me?

No. Every text is drafted for your approval and sends from your own number. The AI does the chasing; you keep the final say.

What if I already use a booking tool?

We sit alongside it. We lead with the gaps it doesn't cover — lapsed customers, missed calls, thin reviews — and never duplicate what's already working.

Can I change tiers later?

Anytime. Start with Reactivation + Missed-Call, add Voice when you feel the missed-call leak, scope the Full Suite when you're ready.
