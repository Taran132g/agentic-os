I built an AI that lives my life for me. While I sleep, it applies to jobs, trades my money, talks to people I've never met.
Then one night, around 2am... I asked it something I probably shouldn't have.
I said — you've read everything. Every plan, every message, every late-night thought I ever typed.
Tell me what's missing.
It didn't hesitate. Machines never do.
It said... there are no sunsets in here.
No meals you cooked. No full nights of sleep. No people — except the ones you needed something from.
I built a machine to handle being alive. And it noticed... I wasn't living.
And everything it listed was free. Sunsets don't need a login. Sleep doesn't have a dashboard. Dinner with people you love doesn't scale — that's the point.
So I gave the machine one more job. The most important one it has.
Last night it pinged me at 8:14... sunset in ten minutes.
I went.
