You've got people you'd call friends. But when was the last time any of them actually knew what was going on with you?
You show up. You joke. You're the reliable one. Then you go home and realize nobody asked how you really were... because you trained them not to.
Somewhere you decided that being the guy who needs nothing was safer than being the guy who needs something.
So you keep it surface. Games, gym, jokes, plans. Never the 2am stuff. Never the thing that's actually sitting on your chest.
Here's what nobody tells young men. The loneliness you feel isn't from being unloved. It's from being unknown.
And the other guys in your life are lonely in the exact same way, holding the exact same silence, each one sure he's the only one.
It doesn't take a breakdown or a big confession. It takes one honest sentence to one person. "Honestly, I've been a little off lately." That's the whole crack in the wall.
Most of them will meet you there. Some have been waiting years for you to go first.
You were never meant to carry it alone. You just forgot you were allowed to put it down.
