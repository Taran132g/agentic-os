Here's a thought you've had and would never admit to anyone: that if people saw all of you, they wouldn't stay.
So you hand them the edited version. The chill one. The easy one. The guy who doesn't need much.
And then you feel alone even in a full room... because nobody there has actually met you.
That's not a flaw in you. Somewhere along the way you learned that love was something you had to earn by being impressive. Low-maintenance. Easy to be around.
So you buried the parts that felt like too much. But the too much was never the problem. The hiding is.
And you're not the only one doing it. Half the room is running the exact same script, waiting for someone else to go first.
You don't undo years of this in a day. You just let one person see one true thing. A real opinion. Something you actually want. Something unedited.
That's not weakness. That's you finding out you were enough as you are the whole time. You just never gave anyone the chance to prove it.
Stop auditioning for a love you already qualify for.
