That same winter, I had to write a research paper for class. Any problem in society. I picked mine in about ten seconds. Why is my generation's self-worth collapsing?
The seven out of ten was still in the back of my head. I didn't connect it. Not yet.
Here's what the research actually says. The feed is not neutral. It's optimized to capture your attention. And the cheapest way to hold human attention is to make you feel like you're not quite enough yet.
Your insecurity isn't a glitch in the system. It is the system. It's the business model working as designed.
One line from that whole project never left me. Before the day even begins, most of us have already decided we are not enough.
I turned in the paper. Got the grade. Felt like an expert. And I still hadn't noticed the most obvious thing about the whole story.
